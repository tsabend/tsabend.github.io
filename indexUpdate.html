About Me

I am a student in Dev Bootcamp's September 15th "Bumblebees" cohort in New York City. I'm originally from Massachusetts and am now living in Brooklyn. I have spent the past four years working as an 8th grade English teacher in the Mississippi Delta and Newark, New Jersey. I'm transitioning into programming with an eye on education technology.

Besides coding, I enjoy playing music, cooking, and fencing.
